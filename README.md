### 💎 Short & Premium Domains
By default, you can instantly register subdomains with **4 or more characters**. 

If you are working on a high-quality project or you would like a **1, 2, or 3-letter domain**, please contact the administrators on our **Discord server**. We manually review these requests to prevent squatting and ensure they go to active projects.

## 🛠️ How to Get Your Domain

1. **Check Availability:** Use our [WHOIS Lookup](https://dash.int.yt/public-whois.php) to see if your desired name is available.
2. **Create an Account:** Sign up at the [Intent Dashboard](https://dash.int.yt/signup.php).
3. **Register:** Claim your domain through the user dashboard.
4. **Connect DNS:** Point your domain NS(nameserver records) to your hosting provider.
   * **Supported:** FreeDNS, Namecheap.
   * **Coming Soon:** Cloudflare support.
